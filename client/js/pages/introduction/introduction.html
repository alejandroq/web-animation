Changing precipitation patterns and more frequent extreme events can threaten the success of hydropower businesses by reducing generating capacity, damaging infrastructure, and exacerbating harmful environmental and social impacts. This tool walks users through a step-by-step process to understand climate-related risks to their project or facility at a high level.

What is the purpose of the tool? The objective of the screening is to identify key risks to existing and planned hydropower projects in order to support further studies, dialogue, and ultimately actions to manage and reduce these risks.

Who are the intended users? The tool is designed for hydropower owners, operators, planners ,investors, and developers in Southeast Asia. No understanding of climate change is necessary.

What resources are required? The tool provides a structured framework, but the thought process that the user undertakes is vital. Therefore, user time and effort are the two most important resources. The tool will typically take a few hours to complete.
